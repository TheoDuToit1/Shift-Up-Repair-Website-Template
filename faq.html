HOW OFTEN SHOULD I SERVICE MY VEHICLE?

+

We recommend servicing your vehicle every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance helps prevent costly repairs and keeps your vehicle running smoothly.

WHAT WARRANTY DO YOU OFFER?

+

All our repairs come with a 12-month warranty on parts and labor. We stand behind our work and ensure your complete satisfaction with every service.

DO YOU USE GENUINE PARTS?

+

Yes, we exclusively use genuine OEM parts for all repairs and replacements. This ensures optimal performance and longevity of your vehicle.

HOW LONG DOES A TYPICAL SERVICE TAKE?

+

A standard service typically takes 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take longer. We'll provide you with an accurate estimate before starting work.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger repairs.

CAN I BOOK AN APPOINTMENT ONLINE?

+

Yes, you can book appointments through our website or by calling our support team. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.

DO YOU OFFER ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 roadside assistance for our customers. Call our emergency hotline and we'll dispatch a technician to your location.

WHAT IF I'M NOT SATISFIED WITH THE SERVICE?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with our service, we'll make it right. Contact us within 7 days for a full review.
